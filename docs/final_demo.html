Honest Scope

This is submission-ready as a research prototype and visual proof of meaningful iteration. The strongest claim is a 3-sample same-family raw latent decode smoke, not broad benchmark generalization.

Next Production Gates

Scale to locked held-out manifests, run multi-seed real training, compare against token-context handoff, then re-open heterogeneous model-family transfer after same-family generalization is stable.

Video Talking Points

"The problem I worked on is whether AI systems can communicate through compact latent states instead of passing long text transcripts. I built an end-to-end LXP prototype with compatibility checks, latent compression, Stage-II alignment training, raw actor decode diagnostics, and visual reporting. The current same-family smoke reaches 100% raw decode exact match on three examples, with 100% extraction and non-degenerate outputs. The limitation is that this is a smoke result, not a production benchmark; the next step is scaling to locked held-out multi-seed evaluations and heterogeneous model-family transfer."
